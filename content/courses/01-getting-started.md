## What students should know first

OpenClaw can feel technical at the beginning, so the first job of your course is to reduce fear. Tell students exactly what they will build, how long it should take, and what success looks like after the first lesson.

## What this module should cover

- What OpenClaw is in plain language
- Who it is useful for
- What a beginner can realistically do in the first hour
- How your course is structured

## Suggested teaching angle

Instead of starting with theory, start with the result:

1. Show the end state.
2. Show the setup path.
3. Show the first useful workflow.

That makes the course feel practical immediately.
