## Goal of this lesson

Your student should finish this lesson with a working environment, not just copied commands.

## Teaching checklist

- Required software
- Local environment setup
- Environment variables
- First boot
- Common errors and how to fix them

## Good course design tip

Whenever a step is easy to break, pair it with a screenshot and a troubleshooting note. That reduces refund requests and support messages.
